PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy discloses how the Icosa and Open Brush organization (collectively aka organization) handles and protects our user’s information.

Our website (aka site) and Open Brush downloadable application (aka app) collects and utilizes personal data from our subscribers, and users of our app for the purpose of customizing and enhancing your experience, and creating personal profiles within the app including logging in to various integrated platforms, as well as knowledge sharing with the community.

Personal Information

Currently the personal information collected includes the user/subscribers;

  • Name

  • Email

  • Social profile URLs

  • Ideas/Opinions submitted via form survey submissions

Personal information is submitted voluntarily and gathered from our public social platforms, forums, internal application and website messaging systems, and submitted forms, and compiled into our Wix and Google databases. This information is used solely within our organization and not sold for any purpose. Some of your information may appear on a public data sheet used to build the community knowledge base and provide resources for our open source project collaboration.

The amount of information you provide is voluntary; however, providing less information might limit your ability to access all of our site’s or application’s features. If you would like your personal information deleted please contact us by using the message bubble at the bottom of the page.

Usage and Analytics Data

We currently do not collect or analyze your personal usage or analytics data.

Cookies

We currently use the hosting website Wix which uses cookies for important reasons, such as: To provide a great experience for visitors. To identify registered members of the site for members only features. To monitor and analyze the performance, operation and effectiveness of Wix's platform. We as an organization do not have access to this information as it is stored by Wix themselves.

Most web browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your web browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Please note, however, that certain features of the site might not function if you delete or disable cookies. THE SITE’S COOKIES DO NOT AND CANNOT INFILTRATE A USER’S HARD DRIVE TO COLLECT ANY INFORMATION STORED ON THE HARD DRIVE.

Communication and Messaging Data

We contact our site visitors and application users by using personal information collected through the avenues mentioned in the “Personal Information” section above. Any communications between you and anyone from our organization are recorded and stored indefinitely for our records. We may contact you to notify you regarding your account, to troubleshoot problems with your account, to resolve a dispute, to poll your opinions through surveys or questionnaires, to send updates about our company, or as otherwise necessary to contact you to enforce our User Agreement, applicable national laws, and any agreement we may have with you. For these purposes we may contact you via email, or social platform messages, or other avenues according to your profile record.

If you would like to opt out of receiving any communications contact us using the message button at the bottom of the website.
